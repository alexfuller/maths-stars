# Maths Stars — Project Brief

> Original brief for the project, captured for future reference. Started in Cowork,
> now continued in Claude Code.

## Purpose

A simple app for my daughter (a girl under 10) to practise her mental arithmetic:
addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

## Core behaviour

- Runs a **practice session** of e.g. 10 questions (the number of questions per
  session must be **tunable**).
- Presents a sum on screen and asks her to **type in the answer**.
- Each session is **timed, but the timer is not shown on screen** (no pressure).
- For each session, retain: the **answers**, **time taken**, **score**, and the
  **date** it was done — for later comparison and progress tracking.

## Levels

The app offers different levels of practice session:

1. Addition of numbers under 10
2. Addition of numbers under 20
3. Subtraction of numbers under 10 — answers should **never be less than zero**
4. Subtraction of numbers under 20 — answers should **never go below zero**
5. Multiplication of numbers up to 12
6. Division of numbers up to 12
7. Mixed test of levels 2 & 4 (addition & subtraction)
8. Mixed test of levels 5 & 6 (multiplication & division)
9. Mixed test of levels 2, 4, 5 & 6

## UI

- Clean and simple, **white background**, with some friendly accent colours
  suitable for **a girl under 10**.
- Big, touch-friendly controls with an on-screen number pad.

## Platform

- A **webapp accessed through the browser**, working equally well on
  **laptop, iPhone, and iPad**.
